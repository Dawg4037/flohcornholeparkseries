What Is FLOH?

The FLOH Cornhole Park Series brings together players of all skill levels at Florida's most beautiful community parks. Every event is a new adventure — new park, new competition, same great community.

🎯

All Skill Levels

Whether you're a backyard beginner or a seasoned thrower, everyone is welcome on the boards.

🌳

Park Series

Each event is held at a different community park across Florida — exploring great outdoor spaces together.

🤝

Community First

Good food, great people, and cornhole. That's the formula. We're building something special here in FLOH.

🏅

Series Points

Compete across multiple events to climb the overall series standings. Every bag counts.

Event #1 Recap

📍 Eagle Lake Park · March 21, 2025

Event #1 at Eagle Lake Park set the bar high. Players spread across the lush green field, cornhole boards lined up end to end, blue skies overhead — exactly what Florida park cornhole should look like. The community showed up, the food was flowing, and the competition was real. We can't wait to do it again.
